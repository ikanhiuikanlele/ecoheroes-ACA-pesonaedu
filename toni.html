WHO IS TONI?

Toni is very good at math and computer science. He enjoys solving problems and designing simple programs in his free time. Because his dad is an electrician, Toni has learned how to fix electrical devices since he was young. Let's see how Toni goes through her day!

WHAT IS YOUR TASK?
