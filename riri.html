WHO IS RIRI?

Riri chooses to ride her bike to school every day. At first, it is just a personal choice, but she soon realizes that biking helps reduce air pollution and saves energy. Even when the weather is hot or she feels tired, Riri stays committed. Let's see how Riri goes through her day!

WHAT IS YOUR TASK?

You will live as Riri, and your task is to choose to correct way to reduce climate change by changing small everyday habits. Have fun!
